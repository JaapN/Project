When scrolling over a country on the map, information about the country name and data will pop up.
The borders of a country will get brightly coloured as well, which will make it clear which country you are looking at. There is a clear contrast between the used fill colours and highlighted border colour.

Click on a country to draw and display the respective educational field distribution barchart below the map. Do note however that data about educational field distribution is not available for all countries, even those for which there is data to colour the respective country with.

- See more at: http://stats.oecd.org/Index.aspx?DataSetCode=BLI
'Education and Training' (share of graduates per field) and 'Social Protection and Well-Being' (Better Life Index).
The data for the educational graduate field distribution is from 2013. The data for the Better Life Index variables is from 2009.
